ABOUT US

Welcome to the AI Hub at the American University of Sharjah (AUS), an innovative initiative by the Center for Innovation in Teaching and Learning (CITL). Our mission is to unlock the transformative power of Artificial Intelligence (AI) to enrich and enhance the educational journey of our educators and learners.

The AI Hub stands as a beacon for innovation, bringing together the latest in AI technology with the timeless values of education. Our initiative is more than just a resource center; it's a gateway to the future of learning, where every member of the AUS community can discover the infinite possibilities that AI brings to education.

OUR VISION

To establish AUS as a regional leader in human-centered AI for higher education, transforming how we teach, research, and engage so our AI-driven efforts enrich lives, sharpen critical thinking, and uphold ethical responsibility.
